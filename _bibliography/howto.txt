For each article, put following as files into repository folder:

*.published.pdf - Main article (published version)
*.preprint.pdf - Main article (preprint/arxiv/accepted version that was submitted)
*.slides.pdf - Presentation slides
*.poster.pdf - Poster
*.external.link - Link to article/main website for this paper
*.repo.link
*.supplementary.link

Also put repo link in BibTex entry  under "code" key
